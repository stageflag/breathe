Dr. Matthias H went before five venture capitalists in the hope of getting funding for My Hearing Hero. As an ear nose and throat doctor (otorhinolaryngologist), he has extensive experience in treating patients with hearing loss. More specifically, those who suffer from sensorineural hearing loss..

In recent years as an otorhinolaryngologist, he has noticed the ever-increasing number of patients suffering from sensorineural hearing loss. Through his long experience in the treatment of precisely these patients, he found the real cause of their suffering.

Therefore, at first glance, he developed a somewhat unusual product. My Hearing Hero hearing aid developed by him is able to eliminate hearing loss within a very short time without the use of medicines or surgery.

How Exactly Does My Hearing Hero Work?

My Hearing Hero is a small electronic device that you wear in or behind your ear. It magnifies sound vibrations entering the ear. Surviving hair cells detect the larger vibrations and convert them into neural signals that are passed along to the brain. That is,it makes some sounds louder so that a person with hearing loss can listen, communicate, and participate fully in daily activities. My Hearing Hero helps people hear more in both quiet and noisy situations.

The sleek and super comfortable design ensures that My Hearing Hero fits patients' ear, and it fit snugly behind ear.
